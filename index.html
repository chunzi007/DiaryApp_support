Frequently Asked Questions

How do I backup my data?
DiaryApp stores your data locally on your device. You can export your data to JSON or Markdown formats via the Settings menu > Data Management section.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your diary entries are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal data. If you enable the AI features, data is processed according to our privacy policy.
Can I use Face ID to lock the app?
Yes, you can enable Face ID / Passcode lock in the Settings > Privacy & Data section to add an extra layer of security to your journal.
